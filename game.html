About Shadow Unit FPS

Shadow Unit FPS is an adrenaline-pumping first-person shooter that immerses you in a world of tactical combat and intense firefights. Join an elite squad to take on challenging missions, battle enemies, and uncover a gripping storyline. With stunning graphics and dynamic gameplay, Shadow Unit FPS offers an unforgettable gaming experience.

Key Features

  • Enemies: Play against offline enemies.
  • Singel-Player Mode: Play offline against enemies around the map.
  • low poly: The graphics fits the game's unique features and the overall vibe you wish to convey.

How to Play

Get started with Shadow Unit FPS by mastering the basics. Here’s a quick guide to help you dive into the action:

  • Movement: Use WASD keys to move, Space to jump, and Shift to sprint.
  • Combat: Left-click to shoot, right-click to aim, and R to reload.
  • Weapons: Switch weapons with number keys (1-4) or scroll wheel.
  • Objectives: Kill all your enemies.
  • Tactics: Play smart, do not die.
